The Real Cause: Clearance + Shock

Let’s look at the actual application:

  • Wheel sorter runs 24/7
  • Packages hit at 2.5 m/s
  • Each hit = lateral impact

The problem isn’t material strength. It’s fit geometry.

  • M8 bolt (shank ≈ 7.8 mm)
  • Mounting hole: 8.5–9.0 mm

That leaves 0.5–1.2 mm clearance. Under vibration? That’s micro-creep hell.

The Fix: Geometry Over Force

① Zero-Fit Shoulder Bolt

  • Shank: 8.00 mm (+0 / -0.02)
  • Matched to H7 hole: 8.05 mm
  • No rattle. No play.

② Custom Alloy + Heat Treatment

  • 42CrMo steel tuned for ductility
  • Handles shock, avoids cracking

③ Load Path Redesign

  • Before: Impact → bolt rattles → fatigue
  • After: Impact → shoulder takes load → zero creep
